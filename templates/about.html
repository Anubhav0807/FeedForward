Our Mission

How Food Forward Began

Food Forward was founded in 2018 with a mission to reduce food waste and bridge the gap between surplus food and those in need.


Starting as a small community effort, we have now rescued over **10 million meals**, working with restaurants, grocery stores, and local farms to ensure food is not wasted.


>Our efforts have expanded nationwide, partnering with shelters, food banks, and schools to provide access to nutritious meals.
